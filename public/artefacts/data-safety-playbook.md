# How we keep your data safe (1 page)

> Forward this to anyone — your accountant, a developer, a partner. No strings.

## Access
- I work through **dedicated service accounts** with the minimum permissions needed.
- **No passwords over chat/WhatsApp.** Access is granted by you, in your own admin panel.
- Access is **revoked cleanly after handover** — you can remove it yourself anytime.

## Data location & scope
- Hosting and databases stay **within the EU**.
- An AI assistant collects **only what's needed** (e.g. enquiry type, topic, contact) — never
  sensitive records. Decisions stay with you/your team.
- Where technically possible, provider settings prevent your data being used to train public models.

## Control & oversight
- **Human-in-the-loop:** nothing sends, publishes or deploys without your approval.
- **Logs on request:** who did what, when — so any review or audit is straightforward.

## Agreements
- For anything touching personal data, we sign a standard **data-processing agreement
  (verwerkersovereenkomst / AVG)** stating what is processed, why, and for how long.

## No lock-in
- Built on tools you already own (Workspace, Notion, Make/Zapier, a VPS).
- You receive a **README** and can hand the system to any developer. This is never a trap.
