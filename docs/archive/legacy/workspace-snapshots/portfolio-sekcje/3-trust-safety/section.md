# Section 3 — Trust & Safety

## Copy (source of truth)

**Eyebrow:** // Risk & safety
**H2:** Built to be safe for a small business.
**Lead:** AI in your business should reduce risk, not add it. Safety isn't a feature I bolt
on at the end — it's part of the architecture from day one.

**For whom (small intro chip line):**
For ZZP and small businesses in NL — services, e-commerce, advisory.

**6 safety cards:**
1. **Human-in-the-loop by design** — Nothing sends, publishes or deploys without your approval. The system proposes; you decide.
2. **Service accounts, not your passwords** — Access goes through dedicated accounts with scoped permissions. No passwords over WhatsApp, ever.
3. **Start on a test inbox** — We can run first on a separate test address (e.g. test@yourdomain.nl) before touching anything live.
4. **EU data, scoped access** — Hosting and data stay in the EU. Access is limited to you and me, and revoked cleanly after handover.
5. **Logged & auditable** — Who did what, when. Logs are available on request so any review or audit is straightforward.
6. **No lock-in** — Built on tools you already own. You get the README and can hand it to any developer — this is never a trap.

**Note:** Want the detail? Ask for the one-page „How we keep your data safe" playbook — yours to forward to anyone, no strings.
