# Section 1 — About: AI Systems Architect

## Copy (source of truth)

**Eyebrow:** Who's behind Quietforge
**H2:** I design systems that code, check and maintain themselves.

**Lead (value proposition — paste verbatim):**
I'm Norbert — an AI Systems Architect. My edge isn't writing code by hand all day; it's
designing intelligent systems that build, verify and keep themselves running, with a human
in the loop so nothing ever breaks your business. I see the whole system, spot the risks and
the hidden debt, design the workflow your AI agents follow — and turn chaos into something
that actually runs.

**Three „what I actually do" cards:**
1. **See the whole system** — I map how your work really flows before touching anything, so automation fits the business instead of fighting it.
2. **Own the risk** — I look for what can break, leak or drift, and design guardrails around it. Safety is part of the architecture, not an afterthought.
3. **Design for AI agents** — I build the rules, context and review gates that let AI do the heavy lifting reliably — not a demo that falls over in week two.

**Background & approach (kills the „no degree" topic without mentioning it):**
I came up through practice, not theory — a dozen real jobs across different industries before
this. That's why I can talk to a plumber and a lawyer in the same afternoon and design a system
that fits how they actually work. For the last few years I've built production systems that
combine AI, automation and human review — including a live multi-agent ecosystem that runs a
real business end to end. Quietforge is that same engine, packaged for businesses your size.

**Working principles (4 chips):**
- Minimal disruption — I build alongside your mess first, replace only when it's proven.
- No lock-in — built on tools you already trust (Google Workspace, Notion, Make/Zapier, a VPS).
- Transparent architecture — every project ships with a diagram + a plain-language description.
- Human in the loop — nothing sends, publishes or deploys without your approval.

**CTA:** Book your Automation Map → /book-discovery/
