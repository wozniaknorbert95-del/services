## Post body (EN — adapt 1–2 lines in your voice)

```text
Your inbox is not a CRM — but it behaves like one when leads sit next to invoices.

Most small business owners I talk to lose roughly half a day every week just triaging email: what is a lead, what is a client, what is noise, what needs a reply today.

The fix is not “let AI read everything and send on autopilot.”

The fix is a supervised workflow:
→ classify incoming mail into lanes
→ draft replies where it helps
→ queue everything behind a human approval gate — you approve every send

I run this live in my Netherlands print business (FlexGrafik): 142 messages per scan, classified and gated before anything goes out.

Fewer missed leads. No autonomous sending. Same architecture I deploy for Dutch ZZP and SME owners through Quietforge.

If this sounds like your week — the proof and the systems are on the site (link in the first comment).
```
