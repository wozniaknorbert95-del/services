> - ✓ A written Automation Map you keep.
> - ✓ A recommended first step, with the numbers behind it.
> - ✓ **€290 — credited toward your project.**

### § C — STEP 2: BUILD
> **Eyebrow:** Step 2
> **H2:** We build it — fast, because it's already proven.
> Your system is adapted from modules that already run a live business, not coded from zero. That's why builds take days, not months. We configure it to your data, your voice and your tools, test it, and hand it over working.

> **What if I only want Step 1?** That's fine — the Automation Map stands on its own, and it's yours to keep.

# 3. `/book` — Book Discovery (the single front door)

**Job:** convert intent into a **paid Automation Map** with minimum friction and maximum trust. Replaces the old "free contact" page (Sitemap §9). This is where cash flow begins.

### Page wireframe
```
HERO  (the offer, plainly)
 → WHAT YOU GET (justifies the fee)
 → HOW IT WORKS (3 micro-steps: pay → book → meet)
 → RISK REVERSAL (credited + keep the doc + no obligation)
 → BOOKING + PAYMENT (calendar + Mollie/Stripe)
 → FORM (qualify lightly)
 → ALT CONTACT
 → MICRO-FAQ
```

### § A — HERO
> **H1:** Start with a paid Automation Map — €290.
> **Subhead:** In 60–90 minutes we find your two or three biggest time-and-money leaks, calculate the ROI, and recommend the right first step. The fee is credited toward your project.
> **CTA (primary, in-page):** `Pay & book your slot →`

### § B — WHAT YOU GET
> **H2:** What you get for €290
> - ✓ A focused 60–90 minute working session.
> - ✓ A written Automation Map — yours to keep, whatever you decide.
> - ✓ Clear ROI on each recommendation.
> - ✓ A recommended first build, with a fixed quote to follow.

### § C — HOW IT WORKS (3 micro-steps)
> **H2:** Three steps to clarity
> 1. **Pay & pick a slot** — secure your session in under two minutes.
> 2. **Tell me about your business** — a short form so I arrive prepared.
> 3. **We meet** — and you walk away with a roadmap, not a sales pitch.

### § D — RISK REVERSAL
> **H2:** No risk, no pressure
> The €290 is credited toward your first project. You keep the written Map either way. And if there's genuinely nothing worth automating, you owe nothing further — that's the honest outcome, and I'll say so.

### § E — BOOKING + PAYMENT
> **Wireframe:** `[ Calendar widget ]  +  [ Secure payment — Mollie/Stripe ]`
> **Copy:** *Choose a time that works for you and confirm with secure payment. You'll get an instant confirmation and a short prep form.*

### § F — FORM (light qualification)
> **Fields:**
> - Name
> - Business name
> - Email
> - Website / link (optional)
> - Your biggest pain *(checkboxes: Drowning in email · Outdated site · Manual quotes · Few leads · Not sure)*
> - Anything else? *(short text)*

### § G — ALT CONTACT
> Prefer to ask something first? `Email me →` · (optional) `Message on Telegram →`

### § H — MICRO-FAQ
> **Why is it paid?** Because it's real work that gives you real value — and it means we both take it seriously. It's credited toward your project.
> **What if I'm not ready to build?** Perfectly fine. You keep the Map and decide in your own time.
> **Is it remote?** Yes — online, wherever you are.

**SEO** — Title: `Book your Automation Map — €290, credited | [BRAND]` · Meta: `Book a paid Automation Map: 60–90 minutes to find your biggest time and money leaks, with ROI and a recommended first step. Fee credited toward your project. Remote.`

*Rationale:* Every element exists to make a *paid* first step feel safe and worth it: clear deliverable, risk reversal, two-minute booking, light qualification. This is the filter-and-fund mechanism (Strategy §5.1) that turns the whole site into cash flow. Payment up front weeds out non-serious enquiries — the opposite of the old free-call leak.
