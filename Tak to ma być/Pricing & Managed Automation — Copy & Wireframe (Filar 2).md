## A.3. Section copy

### § A — HERO
> **H1:** Clear pricing. No surprises.
> **Subhead:** Start small, prove the value, then scale. Every engagement begins with a paid Automation Map — and its fee comes off your first project.
> **CTA:** `Book your Automation Map — €290 →`

*Rationale:* premium calm; price stated up front signals confidence (benchmark: vague quotes are a red flag clients distrust).

### § B — THE PATH
> **H2:** How working together is priced
> ```
> ①  MAP            ②  BUILD              ③  RUN
> Automation Map →  One-time setup     →  Managed Automation
> €290 (credited)   from €1,200           from €99/mo
> ```
> One line: *„Low-risk first step. Pay for the build once. Keep it sharp monthly — only if you want to."*

*Rationale:* turns pricing into a journey (Strategy §5.1), kills "menu paralysis" (your core concern).

### § C — STEP 1: AUTOMATION MAP
> **H3:** Step 1 — Automation Map · **€290**
> A focused 60–90 minute working session plus a written roadmap. We find your two or three biggest time-and-money leaks, calculate the ROI, and recommend the right first build.
> - ✓ Written Automation Map you keep — whatever you decide next.
> - ✓ Clear ROI per recommendation — no vague promises.
> - ✓ **Fee credited** toward your first project.
> - ✓ No obligation to go further.
> **CTA:** `Book your Automation Map →`

*Rationale:* the paid filter that funds your time (Strategy §5.1). "Keep it whatever you decide" removes risk → premium, not pushy.

### § D — STEP 2: BUILD (setup tiers + bundles)
> **H3:** Step 2 — Build your system (one-time)
>
> | System | What it does | From |
> |--------|--------------|------|
> | **Inbox Killer** ★ | Email that sorts, prioritises & drafts | **€1,200** |
> | **Conversion Web Upgrade** | A fast site that captures and converts | **€1,800** |
> | **Lead Magnet Game** | A branded quiz/game that collects leads | **€2,200** |
> | **Sales Funnel Engine** | A step-by-step flow that quotes & books | **€2,400** |
>
> **Bundles (better together):**
> - **Growth** — any 2 systems · **from €3,900**
> - **Pro** — any 3 systems · **from €6,500**
>
> *Final quote is fixed after your Automation Map — priced to the value it creates, never by the hour.*

*Rationale:* Inbox Killer listed first + ★ (spearhead hierarchy). Bundles pull clients up the ladder (Strategy §4.2). "never by the hour" = value-based framing (benchmark).

### § E — STEP 3: RUN (Managed Automation teaser → full page)
> **H3:** Step 3 — Keep it running (optional, monthly)
> Your systems stay sharp as your business changes. Choose the level of care you want — cancel anytime.
>
> | Plan | For | Price |
> |------|-----|-------|
> | **Care** | Keep it healthy & monitored | **€99/mo** |
> | **Manage** | Actively tuned & improved | **€349/mo** |
> | **Partner** | Your ongoing AI operations | **€890/mo** |
>
> **CTA:** `See what Managed Automation includes →`

*Rationale:* MRR is the business (Strategy §5.3). "cancel anytime" = no-lock-in advantage. Full detail lives on the dedicated page (Part B).

### § F — WHY IT'S PRICED THIS WAY (price defence)
> **H2:** Why it's priced this way
> - **You're buying an outcome, not hours.** Pricing reflects the time and money the system saves you — not how long it takes me to build.
> - **Enterprise-grade, small-business price.** My systems do the heavy lifting, so you get architecture usually reserved for big budgets, at a fraction of the cost.
> - **Proven before it reaches you.** Every module already runs a live business — you're not paying to be an experiment.

*Rationale:* directly answers "why not a cheaper freelancer / Fiverr / ChatGPT" (Strategy §8). The AI-workforce moat sold as a *client benefit*.

### § G — PRICING FAQ
> **Is the Automation Map really credited?** Yes — the full €290 comes off your first project.
> **Are there contracts?** No. Setup is one-time; Managed Automation is monthly and you can cancel anytime.
> **Why "from"?** Final setup price is fixed after the Map, once scope and ROI are clear. No surprises after that.
> **Do I have to take a monthly plan?** No. It's optional — most clients add it once they see the value.
> **What about usage costs?** Any third-party/API costs are transparent and itemised — no hidden overages.

*Rationale:* removes every common SMB money-objection (benchmark: hidden costs + lock-in are top fears).

### § H — FINAL CTA
> **H2:** Start with €290 and total clarity.
> **Body:** No big commitment, no guesswork. Book your Automation Map, see the ROI, and decide from there.
> **CTA:** `Book your Automation Map →`

## B.3. Section copy

### § A — HERO
> **Eyebrow:** Managed Automation
> **H1:** Your systems, kept sharp — month after month.
> **Subhead:** Software isn't "set and forget". Your AI worker keeps learning as your business changes — monitored, tuned and improved, so it stays as good on day 300 as it was on day one.
> **CTA:** `Book your Automation Map →`

*Rationale:* "as good on day 300 as day one" = the value of a retainer made concrete. Premium, calm.

### § B — WHY A LIVING SYSTEM NEEDS CARE (reframe)
> **H2:** A living system, not a one-off
> Inboxes change. Offers change. Customers change. A system left untouched slowly drifts out of step with your business. Managed Automation keeps yours aligned, accurate and improving — without you lifting a finger.

*Rationale:* justifies recurring spend by reframing the alternative (drift) as the real risk → sells the retainer without pressure.

### § C — THE THREE PLANS
> **H2:** Choose your level of care
>
> **Care — €99/mo**
> *Peace of mind.*
> - Continuous monitoring & uptime checks
> - Minor fixes & adjustments
> - Monthly health report
>
> **Manage — €349/mo** ★ *most popular*
> *Actively improved.*
> - Everything in Care
> - Active tuning (lanes, drafts, flows)
> - Priority support
> - Small enhancements included
>
> **Partner — €890/mo**
> *Your AI operations, handled.*
> - Everything in Manage
> - Drift monitoring & proactive optimisation
> - New automations as you grow
> - Strategic advisory ("AI COO" on call)
>
> **CTA under table:** `Not sure which? Your Automation Map will recommend one →`

*Rationale:* exactly the three tiers from Strategy §5.3. "★ most popular" anchors the middle (classic premium nudge). Partner = the "AI Ops" / fractional model the market pays €500–2k for.

### § D — WHAT'S ALWAYS INCLUDED
> **H3:** Always included, every plan
> - ✓ Human-in-the-loop — nothing acts without your approval.
> - ✓ Transparent reporting — you see what changed and why.
> - ✓ Your data stays yours.
> - ✓ A real person who knows your system (not a ticket queue).

*Rationale:* baseline trust + the HITL moat repeated. "real person, not a ticket queue" = premium differentiation against agencies.

### § E — NO LOCK-IN
> **H2:** No contracts. No lock-in.
> Stay because it works, not because you're trapped. Cancel anytime — your systems remain yours.

*Rationale:* benchmark shows no-contract positioning wins SMB trust; turns a weakness (solo) into a confidence signal.

### § F — FAQ
> **Do I need a plan?** No — it's optional. But systems improve fastest when they're cared for.
> **Can I change plans?** Anytime, up or down.
> **What if something breaks?** Care and above include monitoring and fixes — usually before you notice.
> **Will it act without me?** Never. Human-in-the-loop is built into every plan.
